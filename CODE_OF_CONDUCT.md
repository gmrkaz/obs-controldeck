# Code of Conduct

OBS ControlDeck is an open-source creator tool. Keep discussions respectful, useful, and focused on improving the project.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful to other contributors.
- Give clear bug reports and feedback.
- Assume good intent.
- Keep issues related to OBS ControlDeck.
- Respect copyright and licensing for sound files and assets.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Spam.
- Publishing copyrighted sound packs without permission.
- Sharing malicious configs or intentionally unsafe presets.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and issues that are abusive, spammy, or unsafe.
